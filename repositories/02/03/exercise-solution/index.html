Strawberry Ice Cream

By Johnny Appleseed

Updated on May 6, 2023

Here's a super easy way to make a tasty ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Whole milk
  • Double cream or heavy cream
  • White sugar
  • A little vanilla extract and salt
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Egg yolk (optional)

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Use a blender to turn strawberries into strawberry purée.

    If you want to make the ice cream a bit more tart, you can add a few teaspoons of lemon juice.

  3. If you want ice cream without egg yolks, simply mix everything together and put the mixture into an ice cream maker. Otherwise, you can continue with the next steps.

  4. Warm the cream and milk in a pan until steaming.

  5. Whisk the sugar and egg yolks for several minutes until the mixture turns yellow.

  6. Slowly pour the hot cream and milk mixture into the pale yellow egg mixture while continuously whisking the contents inside the egg bowl.

  7. Pour the content back into the pan. Use a sieve to filter big particles in the process.

  8. Cook the content over low-medium heat until it slightly thickens.

  9. Pour the cooked mixture onto the bowl, leave it cool for 10 minutes and inside the refrigerator for an hour.

  10. Stir everything together then freeze everything in an ice cream maker.
